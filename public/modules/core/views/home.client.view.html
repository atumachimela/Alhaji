"02" "What you dont know about us & Our Code of Business"

SHARING OUR
VIEWS, EXPERTISE & GOALS


ORIS LINGUISTICS STUDY CENTER deals with corporate and general public clients. Our courses are flexible and tailor-made to suit the needs of our clients. Students receive progress reports and certificate of completion of every level. Our course management procedures includes the following but are not limited to:
  • Lesson attendance and late cancellations per course/individual.
  • Training hour booked delivery and remaining on each course.
  • Learner’s starting level, target level and level achieved to date.
Our goal is to provide high quality education tailored to help of our clients. We publicly declare and decree our code of business ethics and promise to live by what we state in it. We hold ourselves accountable and accessible to our clients and the society at large. We continuously work to ensure that all our efforts support our goal and promise which always has been and always must remain providing unparalleled educational experience for clients. We conduct ourselves with integrity and remain steadfast and respectful in our personal and corporate responsibility.
